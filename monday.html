My three favorite books are Lost Stars, Pride and Prejudice, and A Game of Thrones.

STAR WARS: LOST STARS
Author
Claudia Gray
Year
2015
Synopsis
Set before, during, and after the events of the Star Wars Original Trilogy, childhood friends Ciena Ree and Thane Kyrell end up on opposite sides of the galactic conflict.
PRIDE AND PREJUDICE
Author
Jane Austen
Year
1813
Synopsis
In the early 19th century, Mr. Bennet has five daughters, but his property can only be passed on to a male heir. At least one of his daughters must marry well to support the rest of the family in the event of their father's death.
A GAME OF THRONES
Author
George R.R. Martin
Year
1996
Synopsis
Beginning in the year 298 AC (After Conquest), several noble houses fight for the claim to the Iron Throne. Three different storylines are followed simultaneously and are told from various points of view.
